About PCA Dems

A community of engaged, progressive voters in the Park Cities area and beyond.

Who We Are

Park Cities Area Democrats (PCA Dems) is a community of progressive and independent-minded individuals committed to positive change in our neighborhoods and beyond.

Based primarily in University Park, Highland Park, Greenway Parks, Devonshire, and Bluffview, we warmly welcome participants from all across Dallas.

Our members care deeply about our country and our neighborhoods, want to find people with similar values and passions, and have a desire to turn those passions into actions that make our world better.

What You Can Expect

What We Do

  • Host monthly meetings and social gatherings
  • Create a welcoming environment for like-minded neighbors
  • Partner with organizations in neighboring regions
  • Connect you with Democratic candidates on your ballot
  • Curate independent media resources to keep you informed
  • Provide a central calendar of Democratic events
  • Engage in grassroots voter participation initiatives

What We Don't Do

  • We are not a political action committee (PAC)
  • We do not solicit funds for campaigns or political parties
  • We never share or sell your personal information
  • Our platform is strictly non-commercial
  • Membership is always free (voluntary contributions welcome)

Our Mission

Park Cities Area Democrats represents a community of engaged, progressive voters based primarily in University Park, Highland Park, Greenway Parks, Devonshire, and Bluffview, though we warmly welcome participants from all across Dallas.

We actively engage in grassroots initiatives designed to boost Democratic voter participation across our precincts. From voter registration and block-walking to postcard-writing and phone banking, our members are making a real difference.

"No Drama"

— Liz Kooy, PCDWA Founder
